# LIVE PULSE — Stream Theme

## Concept
"The Planet's Heartbeat" — a 24/7 live data broadcast showing Earth and humanity's real-time stats, styled like a premium news network.

## Visual Identity
- Dark, futuristic news-room aesthetic (#0a0a0f bg)
- Red accent (LIVE label, ticker border) = urgency
- Cyan accent (#00d4ff) = data, technology, space
- JetBrains Mono for numbers = precision
- Inter for text = readability

## Tone
Authoritative but awe-inspiring. We're watching the planet breathe.
No fluff — pure real data, presented beautifully.

## Brand Name
**LIVEPULSE** — the pulse of the living world

## Color Palette
- Background: `#0a0a0f`
- Surface: `#12121a`
- Accent Red: `#ff3b3b`
- Accent Cyan: `#00d4ff`
- Accent Gold: `#ffb800`
- Green: `#00e676`

## Segments Philosophy
Each segment = a different "vital sign" of the planet:
- Space = humanity's reach beyond Earth
- Seismic = Earth's restlessness
- Crypto = the global digital economy's heartbeat
- Solar = the Sun's activity affecting us
- Climate = Earth's changing atmosphere
