# Five Fun Facts About Dogs

Present five fun facts about dogs, one per scene. Make each scene visually distinct — use different components, colors, and layouts for variety. Keep it simple and polished.

The facts:

1. A dog's nose print is unique, like a human fingerprint.
2. Dogs can understand up to 250 words and gestures.
3. The Basenji is the only breed that doesn't bark — it yodels.
4. A Greyhound could beat a Cheetah in a long-distance race.
5. Dogs' sense of smell is 10,000 to 100,000 times more sensitive than humans'.

Discover what's available, then five scenes, one fact each. That's it.
